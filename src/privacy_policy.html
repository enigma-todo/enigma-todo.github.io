Thank you for using Enigma. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our App. Please take a moment to read this policy carefully.

Information We Collect:

Enigma collects and uses the geolocation data of your device solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of the App. We do not collect any personal or sensitive data, such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiable information.

Geolocation Data:

When you use Enigma, we may request access to your device's geolocation data. This data is essential for the proper functioning of the App, as it enables location-based features definitions of the distance between you and the task you created. Your geolocation data is collected only in your device and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.
